AB Control Hub is the ultimate Web-Based tool to test and calibrate PlayStation controllers (DualSense & DualShock 4). Fix analog stick drift, measure input latency, and test buttons online without installing software.

  • Drift Calibration & Center Fix (Permanent)
  • Privacy First: Local Processing (No Uploads)
  • Haptic Vibration & LED Testing
  • Works Offline After First Load (PWA)
