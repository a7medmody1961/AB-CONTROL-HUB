Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Introduction

At AB Control Hub, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our web-based controller calibration tool.

Local Processing (No Data Collection)

AB Control Hub does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All communication with your controller happens locally on your device using the WebHID API. Your button presses, stick movements, and controller serial numbers never leave your browser.

Cookies

We use essential cookies only to store your preferences, such as your selected language and whether you have accepted our terms. These cookies are stored locally on your device.

Advertising and Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party vendors, including Google, to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to AB Control Hub and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Currently, we use Google Analytics to analyze traffic. This service transmits website traffic data to Google servers. We use this data strictly to monitor service stability and user demographics to improve the tool.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
